Frequently Asked Questions

We clarify the main doubts about digital certification to help you make an informed decision.

How does the videoconference work?

The process is simple and secure. You schedule a time, connect via videoconference, and our team validates your data in real-time.

How long does it take to receive?

After the videoconference, your certificate is issued within 15 minutes and sent directly to your email, ready for immediate use.

What documents do I need?

ID, CPF, and proof of residence for e-CPF. For e-CNPJ, company documents such as the articles of incorporation and CNPJ are also required.

Is it secure and legally valid?

Yes, our certificates comply with all ICP-Brazil standards and have full legal validity, accepted by public and private institutions.
